My name is Sam, and I'm a globe-trotting web developer and software engineer (in training). I've trekked through tropical forests and camped in deserts, climbed Kiliminjaro and been chased by Barbary Apes in the Atlas mountains, scuba dived in the Red Sea, slurped soup dumplings at night-market street stalls, and gotten lost in too many cities to count. Currently based in Seattle, my latest adventure is working toward becoming a full-stack developer as a student in the 9th cohort of Ada Developers Academy, a program that trains women and gender diverse people to enter the field of software development.

I believe in the power of tech to transform the way we interact with one other, from neighbors down the street to people across the globe. I am passionate about helping people from all walks of life make connections and explore the world around them through the use of innovative technologies.

Click my portfolio on the navigation to see samples of my work, and feel free to reach out!
